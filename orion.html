Astronomy Facts

Orion is one of the most recognizable constellations in the night sky.

It is named after a hunter in Greek mythology.

Orion contains some of the brightest stars visible from Earth.

ORION

The Orion constellation is easily recognizable by its hourglass shape, with stars like Betelgeuse and Rigel, and the iconic "Orion's Belt," symbolizing a mythological hunter
