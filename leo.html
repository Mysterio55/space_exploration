Astronomy Facts

Leo is one of the zodiac constellations, resembling a lion.

It is visible in the Northern Hemisphere during spring.

Leo contains several bright stars like Regulus and Denebola.

LEO

Leo, the Lion, is one of the zodiac constellations, representing a lion in Greek mythology. It is best known for its brightest star, Regulus.
