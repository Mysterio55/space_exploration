Astronomy Facts

Cassiopeia is a constellation in the northern sky named after the queen in Greek mythology.

It is easily recognizable due to its 'W' shape formed by five bright stars.

CASSIOPEIA

Cassiopeia is one of the 88 modern constellations and is rich in mythology and history.
