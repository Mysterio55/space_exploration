Astronomy Facts

Scorpius is one of the most recognizable constellations due to its distinct shape resembling a scorpion.

It contains several bright stars, including Antares, which is often referred to as the heart of the scorpion.

SCORPIUS

Scorpius is located in the southern sky and is one of the 88 modern constellations.

In Greek mythology, Scorpius was the creature sent by the gods to kill the hunter Orion.
