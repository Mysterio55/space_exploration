Astronomy Facts

Pegasus is a large constellation in the Northern Hemisphere.

It is known for the Great Square, an asterism formed by four bright stars.

Pegasus is named after the mythological winged horse.

PEGASUS

Pegasus, the Winged Horse, is a large and prominent constellation. The Great Square of Pegasus is one of its most recognizable features, formed by stars such as Markab and Scheat.
