Astronomy Facts

Aquila is a constellation located on the celestial equator.

It is often depicted as an eagle and contains the bright star Altair.

Altair is one of the three stars that form the Summer Triangle.

AQUILA

Aquila, Latin for "eagle", is a prominent constellation in the summer sky.
